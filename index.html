Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the creation and application of new ideas, methods, or technologies to drive progress and improvement.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on balancing environmental, social, and economic needs to ensure resources endure for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure comprises essential systems and structures supporting societal, economic, and technological functionality.

Empowerment

Empowerment is enabling people with knowledge, resources, and confidence to make decisions and drive positive change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"To overcome poverty and the flaws of the economic crisis in our society, we need to envision our social life. We have to free our mind, imagine what has never happened before and write social fiction. We need to imagine things to make them happen. If you don't imagine, it will never happen. The developing world is full of entrepreneurs and visionaries, who with access to education, equity and credit would play a key role in developing the economic situations in their countries."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
